About Us


Welcome to K10 Snack and Drinks Store - Your Destination for Refreshment At K10 Snack and Drinks Store, we are your go-to destination for a delightful range of snacks, hot beverages, and cold drinks. Located in the heart of Scarborough, we have been serving our community since 2016, providing a haven for those seeking a quick pick-me-up or a satisfying treat.

Our Mission


Our mission at K10 is simple: to offer our customers a wide variety of delicious snacks and beverages that invigorate the senses and brighten their day. We take pride in being your trusted source for quality refreshments in a welcoming and comfortable environment.

Our Selection


Discover a tempting array of snacks that cater to every palate. From crispy chips and savory pretzels to sweet and indulgent treats, we have something to satisfy every craving. Pair your snacks with our carefully curated hot beverages, including rich coffees, soothing teas, and creamy hot chocolates. For those looking to cool off, our selection of cold drinks, from refreshing iced teas to flavorful sodas, is sure to quench your thirst.

Our Commitment to Quality


At K10, quality is our top priority. We source the finest ingredients for our snacks and take pride in our freshly brewed hot drinks and perfectly chilled cold beverages. Every item on our menu is prepared with care to ensure your taste buds are delighted with every bite and sip.

Our Team


Our dedicated team at K10 Snack and Drinks Store is passionate about providing excellent service and a friendly atmosphere. From our baristas crafting your favorite coffee to our staff assisting you with your selections, we are here to make your visit enjoyable.

Community Connection


K10 is more than just a store; we are an active part of the Scarborough community. We sponsor local events, engage in charitable activities, and work towards making our neighborhood a better place for all.

Visit Us


We invite you to stop by K10 Snack and Drinks Store to explore our wide range of snacks and beverages, find your new favorites, and enjoy a moment of relaxation or a quick refresher. Whether you're on a break from work, meeting up with friends, or simply treating yourself, we're here to serve you.


Thank you for choosing K10 Snack and Drinks Store as your destination for snacks and beverages. We look forward to serving you and brightening your day with our delicious offerings.
